Red Light Yoga Flow

Red Light Yoga Flow is a grounding, restorative movement experience that blends breath-led yoga, intuitive flow, and the supportive power of red light therapy, designed to help the body regulate, restore, and reconnect.

Set under warm red light, the studio becomes a cocoon-like sanctuary that encourages the nervous system to soften while gently activating the body through mindful movement. Red light supports relaxation, circulation, and cellular energy, allowing you to move with intention rather than force.

This class is especially supportive during seasonal transitions and darker months, when energy, motivation, and mood may feel lower. The warmth and glow of red light can help the body feel more regulated and supported when natural sunlight is limited.

⸻

⸻

🔴 Additional Benefits of Red Light Therapy

Red light therapy is widely used in wellness and recovery spaces as a supportive tool for overall wellbeing. When paired with movement and breathwork, benefits may include:

• Encouraging nervous system calm by signaling safety and relaxation to the body
• Supporting circulation and warmth, helping muscles and joints feel more open and easeful
• Aiding post-movement recovery by supporting the body’s natural repair processes
• Supporting cellular energy in a world of constant stress, screens, and stimulation
• Promoting skin vitality and natural glow
• Supporting emotional balance, especially during seasonal changes or low-light months
• Enhancing mind-body connection, making movement feel more integrated and intentional

Red light therapy is a wellness practice and does not diagnose, treat, or cure medical conditions.
