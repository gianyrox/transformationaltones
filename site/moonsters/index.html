Kaitlynn Tassone — Moonsters DAO

Born and raised in Syracuse, Kaitlynn Tassone has dedicated her career to building communities, creating meaningful experiences, and helping people connect with themselves and one another. With over a decade of experience in leadership, entrepreneurship, wellness, and event production, her work has consistently centered on the power of human connection.

Kaitlynn's involvement with Moonsters DAO was rooted not only in a shared vision for the future, but also in lifelong community ties. She grew up in Syracuse alongside Moonsters founder Sol Saver, creating a foundation of trust, shared values, and a mutual passion for bringing people together.

Working with the Moonsters team, Kaitlynn helped support community growth, wellness programming, creative strategy, partnerships, events, and member engagement. Her focus was on helping bridge the gap between emerging technology and real-world connection, bringing wellness, creativity, and authentic relationship-building into the rapidly evolving Web3 and crypto space.

Through virtual events, community activations, wellness experiences, and creative collaborations, she contributed to fostering an engaged and supportive community where innovation and human connection could thrive side by side.

Today, Kaitlynn continues that mission through Transformational Tones, blending sound healing, somatic wellness, music, and community experiences to create transformational spaces for individuals, organizations, schools, healthcare settings, festivals, and corporate teams nationwide.

From Syracuse roots to global communities, Kaitlynn believes that technology is most powerful when it strengthens what matters most—human connection, purpose, and belonging.
