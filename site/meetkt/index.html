Meet Kaitlynn also known as kt the alchemist

A sound therapist, multidimensional artist, and devoted guide.

KT is a certified sound therapist, multidimensional artist, and the founder of Transformational Tones, a sound‑first healing studio rooted in frequency, movement, and art as medicine.

Kaitlynn Tassone, also known as KT the Alchemist, is an artist and entrepreneur who built her life’s work from the ground up guided by lineage, grit, and creative devotion. Her family came to Syracuse, NY from Italy three generations ago, carrying with them a legacy of dedication, community, and self-made resilience that continues to shape everything she creates.

Her grandfather, Dominick Tassone, was a philanthropist and entrepreneur who built his own business and became part of local Syracuse lore through what many still remember as “meatball madness.” While Kaitlynn’s work lives outside the food world, the spirit behind it is the same: build something real, serve the community, and lead with heart. His example showed her that business could be personal, cultural, and deeply human.

Long before studios, stages, or sound baths, Kaitlynn funded her creative vision by knocking doors, learning discipline, leadership, and perseverance firsthand through door-to-door sales. She self-funded every step of her journey, transforming hustle into sovereignty and sales into the engine that powered her artistic freedom. With no investors and no shortcuts, she reinvested her earnings into sound, black light art, movement, and immersive healing experiences.

Starting in 2018, she brought her black light art & sound nationwide traveling across the country doing pop up events in Nashville art galleries, Florida venues, Pennsylvania galleries and studios, colleges, Hollywood stages, festivals, NYC events & immersive experiences bringing all her knowledge and creative energy back to her roots in Syracuse, NY.

Today, her work lives at the intersection of art, music, wellness, and entrepreneurship. Through black light art, spoken word, movement, sustainability and community-driven gatherings her mission is to change the way we look at mental health. She creates spaces where nervous systems soften, creativity reawakens, and people feel safe enough to come home to themselves through art.

Through Transformational Tones and her evolving creative projects as KT the Alchemist, Kaitlynn continues to build bridges between music and medicine, Web 3 technology and humanity, global inspiration and local roots—amplifying Central New York while expanding her work nationwide.

Kaitlynn operates a Brooklyn, NY studio—one of the first wellness studios in New York City to integrate cryptocurrency and Web3 infrastructure—alongside a satellite studio in Syracuse, creating an ecosystem rooted in both innovation and hometown impact. Her work draws from live music culture, meditative sound, and underground electronic influences while remaining grounded, accessible, and community-centered. She is working on releasing music in 2027, with world famous producer, Lederrick, who also produced Lil Peeps "Cry Baby".

Honoring her Italian roots through hard work, creativity, culture, and community, Kaitlynn continues to build in NY & nationwide with the intention bridging old-world values with modern healing, and proving that art can be both sacred and self-sustaining. What began with knocking doors has evolved into an arts-powered future, grounded in legacy and devoted to collective well-being.

Somatic Sound Therapy™ Certification

Why Somatic Sound Therapy™ matters.

KT was certified by Niajae founder of Bliss Bar Wellness and The Sound Therapy Network in a one of a kind training to place Himalayan bowls on the body releasing trapped emotions and relaxing the nervous system. Somatic Sound Therapy, a trauma-informed approach that emphasizes safety, embodiment, and nervous system regulation.
