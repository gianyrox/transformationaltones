The Origin Story

It All Started When…

Sound Bath Sunday™ was placed on KT’s heart during a time when the world felt more disconnected than ever.

Created in 2020 at the height of COVID, Sound Bath Sunday™ began as a virtual experience streamed worldwide by sound therapist and multidimensional artist Kaitlynn Tassone (KT the Alchemist). As isolation became universal, the vision was simple yet profound: to create a Sunday gathering reminiscent of church, where sound replaced sermons and meditation replaced noise.

From living rooms across the globe, participants gathered in stillness, resonance, and shared intention, forming a community through frequency.

“I wanted Sundays to feel like a ceremony again, one built on sound, safety, and authenticity.”

KT, Founder of Sound Bath Sunday™

Broadcast from a small studio to participants around the world, the first Sound Bath Sundays™ became an anchor in a time of uncertainty.

From Screen to Sanctuary

Evolving Into a Live Ritual

As the world reopened, Sound Bath Sunday™ transformed into an in-person experience, immersive gatherings that weave together God, sound, art, movement, and conscious celebration.

Each Sound Bath Sunday™ is intentionally curated to connect spiritually, engage the senses and invite presence, connection, and joy.

Why It Matters

Healing Can Be Joyful

Sound Bath Sunday™ exists as a reminder that healing does not have to be heavy; rest, release, and celebration can coexist.

A lot of people are seeking God but don't always resonate with church due to experiences they may have had that pushed them away. We believe God is found in community curating safe, spiritual spaces.

This is a sober, inclusive space where people gather to reset, connect, and experience joy through sound, art, frequency, spirituality and the love of God.
