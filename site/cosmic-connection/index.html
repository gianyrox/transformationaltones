Illuminate Your Path

Brittany, known as “Realm,” is an intuitive astrologer and Reiki practitioner who weaves Hellenistic and Tropical astrology with spiritual guidance to illuminate your path. With nearly a decade of study and years of experience giving transformative readings, she helps uncover hidden truths and navigate life’s shadowed corners with clarity and compassion. A survivor and thriver, Brittany channels her own life challenges—including living with the rare MCAS condition—into a unique perspective that blends spiritual insight with nervous system awareness. Currently training as a Nutritional Therapy Practitioner (NTP), she continues to expand her ability to guide others toward holistic balance. If you seek deep, honest, and mystical guidance that meets you where you are, Brittany is a trusted guide for your journey of self-discovery and transformation.
