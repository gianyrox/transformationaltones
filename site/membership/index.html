How to choose

A simple way to pick the right level of support

Start by noticing what your body and nervous system are asking for right now: gentle introduction, steady support, or deep ongoing work.

“I’m just beginning”

You’re curious about sound baths and want to gently test how your body responds.

  • Start with a Single Sound Bath or Sound Bath 5-Pack.
  • Layer in 1–2 movement classes monthly if your body wants gentle stretching.
  • Move into Gentle Reset membership when you’re ready for more rhythm.

“I’m ready for steady support”

You want regular nervous system resets woven into your month.

  • Choose Gentle Reset for 2 sound baths per month.
  • Choose Deep Restoration for weekly sound support.
  • Add movement classes as needed for integration and gentle strengthening.

“I’m committed to deep change”

You’re ready to make sound a central pillar in your well-being.

  • Choose Immersive Sound Circle for the deepest, most consistent support.
  • Use supportive movement to help your body integrate big shifts.
  • Add occasional workshops or series for focused themes.

Still unsure? You’re welcome to begin with a single sound bath, then apply a portion of your visit toward a membership within 24 hours.
