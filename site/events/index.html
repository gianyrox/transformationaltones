Event FAQs

Everything you need to know before you join a Transformational Tones event.

Do I need prior experience to attend a sound bath or event?

Absolutely not. All Transformational Tones events are designed to meet you where you are. Whether it’s your first sound bath or your fiftieth, you’ll find a space to relax, release, and reconnect. Simply bring an open mind, and we’ll guide the rest — pillows, blankets, and comfortable seating are provided so you can fully receive the experience.

Who are these events for?

Transformational Tones events are designed for anyone seeking rest, resonance, and transformation through sound, movement, and art. Whether you’re new to sound therapy or a seasoned participant, these gatherings are inclusive, trauma-aware, and suitable for people looking to reset, connect, and expand their sense of presence.

Do I need to be a Transformation Tones member?

No. These events are open to everyone, whether you are a member or new to Transformational Tones. Our goal is to create welcoming, immersive experiences for all participants.

Is there a cost to attend?

Yes. Each event has a participation fee, which supports the creation of high-quality, immersive experiences with curated sound, art, and movement elements for every attendee.

Will Transformational Tones events be recorded?

Yes. Events are occasionally recorded for promotional and marketing purposes. These recordings may be used to share the experience with our wider community or on social media, but individual attendees’ privacy is respected, and no personal details are shared without consent. You can fully immerse yourself in the event without worrying about being singled out.

Our memberships are exclusive and private. In order to be a member with us, you must be approved.
