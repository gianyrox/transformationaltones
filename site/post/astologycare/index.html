Astrology is more than horoscopes

it’s the ancient study of patterns, timing, and energetic rhythm.

There is even scripture on astrology-

Psalm 19:1

“The heavens declare the glory of God;

the skies proclaim the work of His hands.”

Psalm 147:4

“He determines the number of the stars

and calls them each by name.”

Rather than claiming planets control us, astrology operates as a mirror:

  • The moon moves tides - and our emotions.

  • Seasons shape nature - and our behavior.

Astrology is a language of inner and outer alignment, helping us understand cycles of growth, release, and renewal

Why Transformational Tones Cares About Astrology

Because astrology is frequency work -

a natural rhythm that mirrors how sound healing functions in the body.

Astrology gives us:

  • Themes

  • Timing

  • Insight

Sound gives us:

  • Embodiment

  • Release

  • Integration

When the cosmos shifts, our nervous system feels it

and sound is the medicine that helps us process it.

At Transformational Tones, we use astrology not as prediction,

but as orientation - a compass for awareness, ritual, and reclamation.

Healing isn’t something you chase - it’s something you return to.

Astrology gives the context; sound provides the transformation.
