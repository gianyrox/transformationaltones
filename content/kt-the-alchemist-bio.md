# KT The Alchemist

Born and raised in Syracuse with deep roots in Brooklyn, KT The Alchemist is a community builder, sound healing artist, entrepreneur, and creative visionary working at the intersection of wellness, music, technology, and human transformation.

She maintains a home-base studio in Brooklyn while continuing to bring healing back to her hometown community in Syracuse. As a passionate advocate for mental health and nervous system wellness, she hosts transformational sound bath experiences at Branches of Growth—the same place where she once attended therapy herself. What began as a personal healing journey has come full circle, becoming a living example of what is possible when people commit to their growth and healing.

Over the years, KT has traveled across the country studying wellness modalities, investing in personal development, and earning multiple sound healing certifications. Her mission is simple: make healing accessible, experiential, and community-driven.

In partnership with Wellness US, she helps bring wellness experiences, sound therapy, leadership development, and nervous system regulation programs to corporate teams, organizations, conferences, and communities nationwide.

With more than a decade of experience building teams, businesses, and communities, KT has become known for bridging worlds that are often kept separate—bringing together Web3, crypto, immersive art, music, wellness, and conscious entrepreneurship into experiences that inspire connection, creativity, and growth.

Her journey into Web3 led her to work alongside Moonsters DAO founder Sol Saver, a longtime friend from Syracuse. Together with the Moonsters team, she supported community growth, wellness initiatives, partnerships, events, and creative strategy, helping demonstrate how emerging technology can be used to foster authentic human connection and meaningful community.

As the founder of Transformational Tones, KT creates immersive wellness experiences that blend somatic sound therapy, meditation, frequency-based music, visual art, storytelling, and community activation. Her work has included opening festivals, wellness events, and community gatherings with transformational sound bath experiences designed to regulate the nervous system, spark creativity, and create collective moments of healing and connection.

Now expanding into music, KT The Alchemist is developing a signature sound that fuses electronic music, spoken word, meditation, and healing frequencies. Her upcoming releases are being created in collaboration with Lederrick, a producer, songwriter, and artist known for blending genres and creating emotionally immersive musical experiences. Lederrick is recognized for his contributions to the certified Gold album Crybaby by Lil Peep and for his work across alternative and underground music scenes.

Together, they are developing a body of work that combines emotional storytelling, immersive sound design, conscious lyricism, and intentional frequencies—creating music that is both artistically compelling and deeply transformational.

Their collaborative project is scheduled for release in 2027, representing the next evolution of KT The Alchemist's vision: a movement where wellness, music, technology, community, art, and consciousness converge.

Whether leading a sound bath, building a Web3 community, creating immersive art, speaking on mental health, or producing transformational music, KT The Alchemist's mission remains the same: helping people remember their power, reconnect with themselves, and transform their lives from the inside out.
